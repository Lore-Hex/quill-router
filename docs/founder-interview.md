# Founder Interview Workflow

## Goal

Capture founder language so TrustedRouter pages and outreach sound like the founder, not a model.

## Live Interview Prompts

- What should someone understand about TrustedRouter in one sentence?
- Why is "OpenRouter-compatible, but verifiable" the wedge?
- What do you mean by "no prompt logs"?
- What does the trust page prove?
- Who should use this first?
- What would make you approve a migration credit?
- What should we never claim?

## Editing Rules

- Light cleanup only: punctuation, filler removal, and tiny grammar fixes.
- Do not paraphrase claims.
- Keep quote snippets linked to the source transcript.
- Use headings and ordering to make the transcript useful.
- Mark any non-founder connective text as editor notes.

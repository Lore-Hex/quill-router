# Personnel Security And Training Policy

Status: draft for management approval.

Owner: Security Officer.

## Purpose

Ensure personnel and contractors understand TrustedRouter security, confidentiality, privacy, and incident-response responsibilities.

## Scope

Employees, founders, contractors, advisors with production access, and anyone who can access customer data or production systems.

## Policy

- Personnel must sign confidentiality agreements before production access.
- Personnel must receive security training before production access and at least annually.
- Training must cover:
  - Prompt/output no-logging requirements.
  - Secrets handling.
  - Phishing and MFA.
  - Incident reporting.
  - Data classification.
  - Customer legal/compliance sensitivity.
  - Safe debugging.
- Background checks may be required for personnel with broad production access once operationally practical and legally appropriate.
- Departing personnel must return assets and lose access promptly.

## Evidence

- Signed agreements.
- Training records.
- Access approval records.
- Offboarding records.

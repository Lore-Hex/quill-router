# Audit Operations Policy

Status: draft for management approval.

Owner: Security Officer.

## Purpose

Maintain SOC 2 readiness evidence and ensure public compliance statements stay accurate.

## Policy

- Compliance documents are version controlled.
- Evidence is collected before Type I and periodically during Type II.
- Public pages must not claim SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001, or signed DPA/BAA until evidence exists.
- Audit evidence must avoid customer prompt/output content.
- Exceptions must be recorded with owner, rationale, and remediation date.
- Management reviews control status at least quarterly.

## Evidence Collection

Evidence should be stored in a restricted folder or GRC tool with:

- Evidence ID.
- Control ID.
- Owner.
- Date collected.
- Source system.
- Description.
- Link or attachment.
- Reviewer and review date.

## Type I Workflow

1. Confirm auditor scope.
2. Approve policies.
3. Collect point-in-time evidence.
4. Resolve obvious exceptions.
5. Freeze evidence package.
6. Provide management assertion and representation letter to auditor.

## Type II Workflow

1. Define observation period.
2. Run recurring controls on schedule.
3. Record every access review, vendor review, incident, vulnerability review, change review, and risk review.
4. Track exceptions continuously.
5. Provide evidence to auditor at period end.

# Risk Management Policy

Status: draft for management approval.

Owner: Security Officer.

## Purpose

Identify, assess, track, and mitigate risks to TrustedRouter security, availability, confidentiality, privacy, and processing integrity.

## Risk Categories

- Prompt/output confidentiality.
- Attestation and trust publication.
- API key and BYOK secret handling.
- Billing and credit ledger correctness.
- Provider availability and provider privacy posture.
- Cloud infrastructure and regional availability.
- Vendor/subprocessor dependencies.
- Employee/contractor access.
- Compliance and legal misstatement.
- Abuse, fraud, and rate limiting.

## Procedure

1. Identify risk from incidents, reviews, audits, product changes, vendor changes, or customer requests.
2. Record risk in the risk register.
3. Rate likelihood and impact.
4. Assign owner and mitigation.
5. Set target date.
6. Review at least quarterly.
7. Close only when evidence confirms mitigation or risk is formally accepted.

## Risk Ratings

- Critical: could cause prompt/output exposure, material outage, material financial loss, or regulatory breach.
- High: likely customer impact or material control weakness.
- Medium: limited customer impact or meaningful operational risk.
- Low: minor or theoretical risk.

## Risk Acceptance

Risk acceptance requires owner, rationale, compensating controls, expiration/review date, and management approval.

## Evidence

- Risk register.
- Quarterly review notes.
- Mitigation tickets.
- Risk acceptance records.

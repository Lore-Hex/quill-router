Lore Hex Corp management is responsible for designing, implementing, operating, and maintaining controls over the TrustedRouter hosted service.

Selected categories:

- Availability.
- Confidentiality.

- Processing Integrity for billing, authorization, settlement, credits, refunds, and routing correctness.

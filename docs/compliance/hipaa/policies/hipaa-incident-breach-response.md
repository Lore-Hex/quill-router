# HIPAA Incident And Breach Response Policy

Status: draft for HIPAA readiness.

Owner: Security Officer.

## Purpose

Define how TrustedRouter identifies, investigates, and reports incidents involving PHI/ePHI.

## Scope

Any suspected unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, loss, or unavailability of PHI/ePHI processed by TrustedRouter or an approved subprocessor.

## Procedure

1. Create incident record and classify as HIPAA-relevant.
2. Preserve request IDs, timestamps, workspace IDs, route/provider, logs, and deployment evidence.
3. Avoid collecting or spreading PHI during investigation.
4. Contain by disabling keys, route providers, content export, or affected systems as needed.
5. Assess whether PHI was acquired, accessed, used, or disclosed contrary to the BAA or HIPAA requirements.
6. Notify the customer according to the BAA.
7. Support customer breach-risk assessment and regulatory obligations.
8. Remediate root cause and document corrective actions.

## Minimum Record

- Date/time detected.
- Reporter.
- Affected customer/workspace.
- PHI involved or suspected PHI category.
- Systems/providers involved.
- Containment actions.
- Notification decisions.
- Customer communications.
- Root cause.
- Remediation.
- Closure approval.

## Evidence

- Incident record.
- Timeline.
- Customer notices.
- Post-incident review.
- Corrective action tracking.
